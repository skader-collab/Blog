About Hijack Guardian Ciphers

Bridging Liberia to Global Cybersecurity Excellence

Our Story

Founded in Monrovia, Liberia, Hijack Guardian Ciphers (HGC) emerged from a vision to transform cybersecurity awareness and protection across West Africa. Starting from our headquarters at 72nd SKD Boulevard, we've grown from a local initiative into an international cybersecurity force, while maintaining our commitment to Liberian digital development.

Our mission extends beyond national borders, combining Liberian innovation with global expertise to create a safer digital environment for all.
